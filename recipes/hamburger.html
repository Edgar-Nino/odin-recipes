Hamburger

Description

Get ready to sink your teeth into the juiciest and most delicious homemade hamburger! This classic hamburger recipe will have your taste buds dancing with joy. Whether you're firing up the grill for a backyard BBQ or simply craving a mouthwatering burger, this recipe won't disappoint. It's been a favorite among burger enthusiasts for years and continues to be a go-to choice for burger lovers everywhere.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef: You'll need high-quality ground beef for the best flavor and texture.
  • Burger Buns: Select your favorite type of hamburger buns to hold your scrumptious patty.
  • Onion: Finely chop a fresh onion to add a burst of flavor to the burger.
  • Cheese Slices: Choose your favorite cheese slices to melt over the burger.
  • Lettuce and Tomato: Fresh lettuce leaves and ripe tomato slices provide a refreshing crunch.
  • Pickles: Tangy pickle slices give the burger an irresistible zing.
  • Ketchup and Mustard: Classic condiments that add a perfect touch of sweetness and tanginess.
  • Salt and Pepper: To season the patties to perfection.

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, finely chopped onion, salt, and pepper. Mix gently until well combined, being careful not to overwork the meat.
  3. Shape the meat mixture into burger patties of your desired size and thickness.
  4. Grill or cook the patties on the stovetop over medium-high heat to your preferred level of doneness.
  5. If desired, place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to allow it to melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a separate pan until lightly browned.
  7. Assemble your burger by placing a cooked patty on the bottom half of the bun.
  8. Add lettuce, tomato slices, and pickles on top of the patty.
  9. Squeeze ketchup and mustard over the toppings.
  10. Finish by placing the top half of the bun on the condiments to complete your delicious hamburger.
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy your mouthwatering homemade burger!
