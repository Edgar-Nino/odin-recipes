Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Indulge in the rich and creamy goodness of Spaghetti Carbonara, a classic Italian pasta dish that's quick and easy to make. This recipe combines perfectly cooked spaghetti with a luscious sauce made from eggs, grated cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. Each bite delivers a burst of flavors and a comforting experience that will leave you wanting more. It's a fantastic option for a satisfying weeknight meal or a special dinner with loved ones.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti: 400g of high-quality spaghetti pasta.
  • Eggs: 4 large eggs, at room temperature.
  • Pancetta: 150g of pancetta or guanciale (Italian cured pork jowl), diced.
  • Parmesan Cheese: 1 cup of freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
  • Black Pepper: Freshly ground black pepper to taste.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt for seasoning.
  • Olive Oil: 2 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil.
  • Garlic: 2 cloves of garlic, minced (optional, for added flavor).

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain, reserving a cup of pasta water, and set aside.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and a generous amount of freshly ground black pepper. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced pancetta and cook until it becomes crispy and golden brown.
  4. If using garlic, add it to the skillet with the pancetta and saut√© for a minute until fragrant (optional).
  5. Remove the skillet from heat.
  6. Immediately add the cooked and drained spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta.
  7. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the hot spaghetti while tossing continuously. The residual heat will cook the eggs and form a creamy sauce.
  8. If the sauce seems too thick, add a little of the reserved pasta water gradually until you reach the desired consistency.
  9. Season with a pinch of salt, if needed (remember that the pancetta and cheese are already salty).
  10. Garnish with extra grated Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of black pepper.
  11. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Carbonara!
